    About Me

    Hello! Welcome to my page. My name is Frankie Hickman, I'm late in life to start a new career. I have been an opera singer with the Houston Grand Opera for 21 years. I've been a private voice/music/choral director for 25 years. I'm a mom of two beautiful young adults. I chose to pursue this career for my final attempt at success. As a singer, I was fortunate to go to Italy to sing. I spent time in Aspen, Cincinnati and Miami as a singer, but as I matured, I wanted kids, so I settled in Houston and started singing the the chorus of the Houston Grand Opera. My goal is to find a challenging job to serve my needs as a successful and contributing member of a dynamic and adventurous team.
